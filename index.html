About Element Fabricating

Element Fabricating was established by Wade Gagnier.

Wade built his career in the early 90’s rigging boats for a local (Coeur d’Alene, ID) dealership and marine repair shop (Mark’s Marine). After working many years at Mark’s building his way up from rigger, to Yamaha and Johnson certified mechanic, and finally to co-owner, Wade decided it was time to move on and focus on his creative abilities building custom aluminum and steel products.

With our solid knowledge of the competitive fishing industry we have the ability to build anthing you can imagine for your boat so you can earn your competitive edge, from towers, planer boards, to down rigger brackets we have you covered.

Along with an extensive background in the marine industry we also market to the commercial and residential customers with our custom steel and aluminum railing systems, chimney caps, stair systems, and unique architectural interior design.
